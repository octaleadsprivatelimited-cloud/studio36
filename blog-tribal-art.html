Tribal Art and Culture: Documenting Chhattisgarh's Heritage

Chhattisgarh is home to over 40 tribal communities, each with unique artistic traditions. Studio 36 is committed to documenting and preserving these cultural treasures through professional filmmaking.

Dhokra Metal Craft

The lost-wax technique used by Bastar artisans creates stunning metal sculptures. We've documented master craftsmen creating intricate pieces, preserving this 4,000-year-old technique for future generations.

Gond and Pithora Paintings

Vibrant tribal paintings depicting folklore, nature, and daily life. Our documentaries showcase artists at work, explaining symbolism and techniques that make each painting a masterpiece.

Bamboo and Wood Crafts

From functional baskets to decorative items, tribal bamboo craftsmanship is sustainable art. We've created content highlighting these eco-friendly practices and promoting artisan communities.
