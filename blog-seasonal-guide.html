Best Times to Film in Chhattisgarh: A Seasonal Guide

Chhattisgarh experiences distinct seasons, each offering unique filming opportunities. Here's our comprehensive guide to planning your shoot based on seasons.

Monsoon Magic (July-September)

Waterfalls like Chitrakote and Tirathgarh are at their most spectacular. Lush green landscapes perfect for nature content. Challenges include rain protection for equipment and accessibility to some locations.

Winter Wonder (November-February)

Pleasant weather makes it ideal for outdoor shoots. Clear skies and comfortable temperatures. Perfect for extended outdoor filming sessions and festival documentation. This is peak season for weddings and corporate events.

Summer Opportunities (March-May)

Early mornings and late evenings offer soft light. Midday heat requires indoor shoots or shaded locations. Great for capturing the authentic summer lifestyle and agricultural activities. Mango season adds color and cultural relevance.

Festival Calendar

Plan around major festivals - Bastar Dussehra (September-October), Hareli (July-August), and Teeja (August). These festivals provide spectacular content opportunities with vibrant colors, traditional attire, and cultural performances.

Studio 36 operates year-round with climate-controlled studios for any season. Contact us for season-specific filming guidance!
