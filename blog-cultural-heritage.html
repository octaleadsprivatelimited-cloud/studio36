Chhattisgarh's Cultural Heritage Through Our Lens

Chhattisgarh's cultural heritage is rich, vibrant, and deeply rooted in tribal traditions. At Studio 36, we've had the privilege of documenting this heritage through our films and videos, preserving ancient art forms for future generations.

Traditional Dance Forms

Panthi and Raut Nacha are iconic dance forms of Chhattisgarh. The energy, colorful costumes, and rhythmic movements make them visually stunning on camera. We've documented numerous performances, capturing the essence of these centuries-old traditions.

Tribal Art and Crafts

From Bastar's metal crafts to Kondagaon's wooden toys, tribal artisans create masterpieces. Our documentaries showcase their techniques, preserving knowledge that has been passed down through generations. These films serve both as cultural records and promotional content for artisan communities.

Festivals and Celebrations

Madai festivals, Dussehra celebrations in Bastar, and Hareli are spectacular events. We capture the fervor, traditions, and community spirit that define these celebrations, creating content that resonates with both local and global audiences.

Folk Music Documentation

Chhattisgarhi folk music, sung in local dialects, tells stories of daily life, love, and nature. We've produced several music videos featuring traditional instruments like the timki and manjira, helping preserve and promote these musical traditions.

Our Cultural Mission

At Studio 36, documenting Chhattisgarh's cultural heritage isn't just work—it's our responsibility. Through professional filmmaking, we ensure these traditions are preserved digitally and shared with the world, inspiring pride in our rich cultural legacy.

Partner with Studio 36 to document your cultural events, traditional arts, or heritage projects. Let's preserve Chhattisgarh's cultural wealth together!
