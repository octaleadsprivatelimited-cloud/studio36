Studio 36: Bringing Bhilai's Stories to the Screen

Located in the heart of Smriti Nagar, Bhilai, Chhattisgarh, Studio 36 has been at the forefront of transforming local narratives into compelling visual stories. Since our inception, we've been committed to showcasing the rich cultural tapestry and modern dynamism of Bhilai and the broader Chhattisgarh region.

Our Vision for Bhilai

Bhilai, known primarily for its steel industry, has stories that deserve to be told on a grander scale. From the bustling streets of Nehru Nagar to the serene landscapes surrounding our city, every corner has a narrative waiting to unfold. At Studio 36, we believe in capturing these authentic moments and transforming them into cinematic experiences.

What Sets Us Apart

Being locally rooted in Bhilai gives us an unique advantage. We understand the pulse of our city, the cultural nuances, and the stories that resonate with our community. Our team combines technical expertise with deep local knowledge to create content that is both professionally crafted and culturally authentic.

Services We Offer

  • Short Film Production
  • Corporate Video Creation
  • Vlog Production and Editing
  • Documentary Filmmaking
  • Event Coverage
  • Social Media Content Creation

Our Impact on Local Content Creation

Over the years, Studio 36 has produced over 150 films and vlogs, accumulating more than 20 million views. We've helped local businesses tell their stories, documented cultural events, and given voice to untold narratives from Chhattisgarh. Our work has inspired a new generation of content creators in Bhilai and surrounding areas.

The Future of Filmmaking in Bhilai

As Bhilai continues to grow and evolve, so does its potential as a filmmaking destination. We're excited to be part of this journey, pushing creative boundaries and setting new standards for video production in Central India. Whether you're a business looking to enhance your brand presence or an individual with a story to tell, Studio 36 is here to bring your vision to life.

Visit us at our studio in Smriti Nagar, Bhilai, and let's create something extraordinary together.
