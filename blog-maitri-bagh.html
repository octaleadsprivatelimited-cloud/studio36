Exploring Maitri Bagh: A Filmmaker's Paradise

Maitri Bagh, Bhilai's crown jewel, is more than just a zoo and garden complex. It's a filmmaker's dream location offering diverse backdrops within a single venue. From the serene musical fountain to the vibrant animal enclosures, this Indo-Russian friendship park provides endless creative opportunities.

The Musical Fountain

The dancing waters synchronized to music create magical visuals, especially during evening shows. Perfect for romantic scenes, music video sequences, or establishing shots that capture Bhilai's modern amenities. The colorful lights transform the space into an ethereal setting.

Natural Backdrops

Lush green lawns, tree-lined pathways, and carefully landscaped gardens offer natural beauty within city limits. Ideal for family vlogs, pre-wedding shoots, and nature-themed content. The park's mature trees provide natural shade and beautiful dappled lighting.

Wildlife Filming Opportunities

The zoo section houses various species, providing opportunities for educational content and wildlife documentation. With proper permissions, you can create engaging content about conservation, animal behavior, and environmental awareness.

Best Shooting Times

Early mornings offer soft light and fewer crowds. Late afternoons bring golden hour magic. Evening musical fountain shows are spectacular but require planning for crowd management. Studio 36 can help secure necessary permissions and plan optimal shoot schedules.

Our Maitri Bagh Projects

It's a favorite spot for local content creators and we're always discovering new angles and creative possibilities.
